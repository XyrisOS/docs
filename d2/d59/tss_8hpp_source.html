26 /**
27  * @brief The Task State Segment (TSS) is a special data structure for x86 processors
28  * which holds information about a task. The TSS is primarily suited for hardware
29  * multitasking, where each individual process has its own TSS. In Software multitasking,
30  * one or two TSS's are also generally used, as they allow for entering
31  * Ring 0 code after an interrupt. (OSDev Wiki)
32  *
33  * Thanks to OSDev Wiki for this section of code.
34  *
35  */
